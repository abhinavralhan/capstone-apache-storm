Dashboard for Twitter Analytics



Our objective is to take any hashtag as an input and perform different analystics on tweets. A hashtag is a label used in social networks that makes it easier to identify tweets of a specific theme.
The first bolt calculates the sentiment of a particular tweet and gives three outputs - good , neutral and bad.
The second bolt calculates how many times a tweet has been retweeted.
The third bolt calculates how many countries are talking about a particular hashtag.
